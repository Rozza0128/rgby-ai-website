Sector

Evidence-led monitoring for damp, mould, resident safety, and housing compliance

Supporting landlords and housing providers with structured compliance evidence, environmental monitoring, and regulatory control.

The challenge

Social housing providers face increasing regulatory pressure under Awaab's Law and HHSRS frameworks. Damp and mould complaints are rising, but current approaches rely on reactive inspections and fragmented data. Without structured monitoring, providers struggle to demonstrate compliance, identify systemic issues, or evidence their response.

Where RGBY fits

RGBY provides continuous environmental monitoring, causal analysis, and structured compliance outputs.

  • Environmental signal monitoring
  • Damp and mould risk scoring
  • Compliance evidence generation
  • Resident safety assurance
  • Regulatory reporting support
  • Drift detection for degrading conditions

Typical outputs

  • Environmental risk assessments
  • Compliance evidence packages
  • Trend analysis and early warning reports
  • Audit-ready decision trails

Why deterministic logic matters here

In housing, compliance evidence must be defensible. Probabilistic outputs create ambiguity in regulatory proceedings. RGBY's deterministic approach ensures that every assessment is reproducible, traceable, and legally robust.
