Sector

Control monitoring, decision traceability, and audit support for financial services

Structured oversight for portfolio compliance, regulatory reporting, and operational control assurance.

The challenge

Financial services firms operate under extensive regulatory frameworks including MiFID II, FCA conduct rules, and increasingly complex ESG obligations. Manual compliance processes struggle to keep pace with the volume and velocity of decisions that require oversight.

Where RGBY fits

RGBY provides structured monitoring of control effectiveness, decision traceability, and compliance evidence across portfolio and operational contexts.

  • Portfolio compliance monitoring
  • Control effectiveness assessment
  • Decision traceability
  • MiFID II and FCA audit support
  • Regulatory reporting structure
  • Operational risk signal detection

Typical outputs

  • Control assurance reports
  • Decision audit trails
  • Regulatory submission evidence
  • Risk signal dashboards
  • Compliance gap analysis

Why deterministic logic matters here

In financial services, regulatory scrutiny requires that every decision can be traced to its inputs. Deterministic logic ensures that compliance assessments are reproducible and defensible under examination.
