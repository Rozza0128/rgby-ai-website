Sector

Structured reasoning and decision support for defence and security environments

Assurance workflows, procurement integrity, and auditable decision traces for sensitive operational contexts.

The challenge

Defence and security environments require decisions that are traceable, justified, and aligned with policy frameworks. Procurement processes, safety cases, and operational decisions carry significant consequences when control frameworks fail.

Where RGBY fits

RGBY provides structured reasoning support for complex decision environments, maintaining audit trails and assurance evidence across procurement, operational, and safety contexts.

  • Structured reasoning for complex decisions
  • Procurement integrity monitoring
  • Safety case evidence
  • Assurance workflow support
  • Decision trace documentation
  • Policy alignment verification

Typical outputs

  • Decision audit trails
  • Procurement integrity reports
  • Safety case evidence packages
  • Assurance documentation
  • Policy compliance assessments

Why deterministic logic matters here

In defence contexts, decisions must be explainable and reproducible. Deterministic outputs ensure that every assessment can be reviewed, challenged, and verified without ambiguity.
