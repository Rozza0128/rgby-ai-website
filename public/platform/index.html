Platform

A deterministic reasoning and monitoring layer for complex operations

RGBY combines signal detection, structured reasoning, drift monitoring, and auditable outputs to help organisations maintain control in regulated and high-consequence environments.

What the platform does

RGBY is designed for environments where compliance, safety, and operational integrity are non-negotiable. It provides a structured layer for interpreting signals, monitoring change, detecting drift, and producing outputs that are traceable and auditable.

Core capabilities

Signal Detection

Continuous monitoring of environmental, behavioural, and procedural signals across operational domains.

Drift Monitoring

Tracks how conditions evolve over time, identifying deviations from expected operational trajectories.

Deterministic Scoring

Outputs are produced through deterministic logic, not probabilistic inference. Every result is reproducible.

Decision Traceability

Complete audit trails from signal to score to action. Every output can be traced to its inputs.

Evidence-Ready Outputs

Produces outputs structured for regulatory review, legal proceedings, and compliance reporting.

Edge / On-Prem / Sovereign Deployment

Deployable at the edge, on-premises, or in sovereign environments. No mandatory cloud dependency.

How it works

The platform maps operational events into a four-part state space — risk, pattern, procedure, and behaviour. By tracking how these dimensions change over time, RGBY detects drift before it becomes failure. The CNVF Vector Engine applies deterministic logic to assess whether the system's trajectory remains operationally plausible.

Why deterministic logic matters

Probabilistic systems produce different outputs for the same inputs. In regulated environments, this creates audit risk and erodes trust. RGBY's deterministic approach means that the same inputs always produce the same outputs — making every decision explainable, reviewable, and defensible.
