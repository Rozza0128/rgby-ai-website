Why damp and mould is a causation problem

Current approaches to damp and mould focus on detection and complaint response. But detection without causation analysis leads to repeated interventions, wasted resources, and ongoing risk. DewPoint Guardian is being built to close this gap — connecting environmental signals to root causes, and root causes to evidence-led mitigation.

What the system is being built to support

  • Environmental signal monitoring and risk scoring
  • Causal analysis linking conditions to root factors
  • Mitigation pathway identification and tracking
  • Compliance evidence generation for regulatory review
  • Resident safety assurance and communication support
  • Integration with existing housing management systems

Edge-first. Sovereign. Low-power.

DewPoint Guardian is designed for edge deployment — processing data locally without cloud dependency. Sub-3W power draw. Patent-protected hardware. Deterministic scoring at the point of measurement.
