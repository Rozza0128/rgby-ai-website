How we think

Four channels.
One framework.

Every signal gets classified. Every output gets an audit trail. The RGBY framework is the backbone of everything we build — encoding intent, risk, and confidence into every layer.

Deterministic over probabilistic

Core decision paths are reproducible and auditable. No black boxes, no "it depends." The same input always produces the same output.

Governance as architecture

Compliance isn't bolted on after — it's built into the system from day one. Every pipeline has guardrails, kill switches, and audit chains.

Explainability by default

If the system can't explain why it made a decision, it doesn't make one. Every output comes with provenance and reasoning.

Built for the real world

Not demos. Not proofs of concept. Production systems that handle messy data, edge cases, and the pressure of regulated environments.

Who we are

Built in Birmingham.

Not Silicon Valley. Not London. Birmingham.

"The AI industry has a trust problem. Everyone's building systems that are impressive in demos and terrifying in production. We started RGBY because we believe the next generation of AI shouldn't just be powerful — it should be accountable."

RGBY.ai was born from a simple observation: the more capable AI gets, the less we can explain what it's doing. That's fine for generating images. It's not fine when the output affects people's lives, livelihoods, or legal standing.

We build from Birmingham because great technology doesn't need a famous postcode. It needs rigour, conviction, and the kind of no-nonsense approach that this city was built on. We're not here to move fast and break things. We're here to build things that don't break.

The EU AI Act is here. Are your systems ready?

New regulations require AI systems to be transparent, auditable, and explainable. RGBY was designed for exactly this — governance-grade AI that meets compliance requirements by architecture, not afterthought.
